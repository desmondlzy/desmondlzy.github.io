Research Experience

Currently at IST Austria, I am working on a new way to recreate a digital photo in the physical world using a programmable embroidery machine.

Starting from my final year at CUHK, I worked with Prof. Fu Chi Wing, on a fabrication project as my graduation project. We studied the design and optimization of the structural rigidity of assembly structure.

In year 2020, I received Professor Charles K. Kao Scholarship and worked with Prof. Anand Sarwate at Rutgers University, on computation efficient neural networks (remotely, due to COVID). Before that, I’ve also explored generating adversarial samples in NLP with Prof. Michael Lyu.
