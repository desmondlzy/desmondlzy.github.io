About My PhD School Application

July 18, 2022

I will begin with some background of this post: I saw a senior student in the EPFL IC slack suggest the new admits write something about their application. I think it’s a good idea. The grad school application to me, the same as to many others, was more or less a stressful thing.

There are way too many materials to polish; way too many deadlines to keep track of; way too many factors to think about.

I’ve read lots of articles by academia people on why and how pursuing a PhD.

Let’s take a divide-and-conquer strategy here; decompose the whole PhD application process in the following components and discuss each one of them.

  • Making up mind going for a PhD
  • Standardized exams
  • Application essays
  • Resume

Making up mind going for a PhD

I don’t know if you would be surprised by this part. This is essentially to throw this question to yourself.

Am I really sure that I want to do a PhD?

It might seem somewhat redundant; just say yes and we are good to go. But beyond a quick yes or no, I feel there is way more to think about here. And I believe that with a prepared mindset, it might be a lot easier to figure out everything you run into on your journey to PhD.
